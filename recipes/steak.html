Steak and Potatoes

Description

This is the perfect steak and potatoes recipe for gym goes looking for a easy to make, time efficient, nutrient dense meal.
Simple meals like this are the best way to staying consistent with your dieting and this meal I have found especially benifitial.
I hope you enjoy this meal as much as I do, and I hope you can find some similar benefits.

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Rosemary/Thyme
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Veges of choice
  • Aioli/Sourcream

Steps

  1. Wrap potatoes in foil
  2. Place on backing tray on cook for 60 mins at 180 degrees celcius
  3. Take potatoes out and leave them in foil
  4. Heat and oil pan
  5. Add a layer of salt to both sides of your steak
  6. Also add a pinch of pepper to both sides
  7. Cook both sides of steak for 3-4 mins
  8. Once crust has formed on both sides, add a wad off butter to pan
  9. Add garlic *chopped* and rosemary to the melted butter
  10. Use a spoon to apply the baste to the steak
  11. Once most of it has been absorbed, take steak off heat
  12. Take foil off potatoes, cut them in half
  13. Add butter to the middle, salt and pepper
  14. Serve with veges + sauces off choices
  15. Enjoy!
