Banana Smoothie

This is a simple recipe for a banana smoothie,
this is a great to get some extra protein, and healthy fats into your day.
I like to add protein powder, although it can diminish taste, I personally still enjoy it alot.
For gym goers I highly receommend chobani's protein yoghurt!
Remember you can always add more ingrdient as you blending, depending on texture, taste ect.

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Peanut butter
  • Yohgurt
  • Honey
  • Milk

    • Steps

    1. Add 3tbps of yohgurt to blender
    2. Add 1tbps of peanut butter
    3. Chop up banana and add to blender
    4. Add honey
    5. Blend and Enjoy!
