Eggs on Toast

Description

This is a recipe for the eggs on toast I have most mornings,
once again this is a meal that has helped me stay consistent with my eating.
It's quick, easy to make and is very convient when mornings are a rush.
For gym go-ers I really recommend using burgen soy-lin bread as it's high in protein.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Avocado
  • Butter
  • Bread
  • Salt + Pepper
  • Tobasco

Steps

  1. Put your toast down
  2. While toast is down prepare your eggs
  3. Crack eggs into bowl, add a cap of milk, and whisk
  4. While toast is still down, heat pan and add butter
  5. Once toast pops add mixture to pan
  6. Use a mixing spatchula to perform a "dragging" motion towards yourself
  7. Once eggs are nice and scrambled, take off heat
  8. Add avovado to toast
  9. Place eggs on top
  10. Add salt, pepper and tobasco
